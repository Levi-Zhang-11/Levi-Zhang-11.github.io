SmartFCT: Improving Power-Efficiency for Data Center Networks with Deep Reinforcement Learning

C.N. Patent, 2020.

Reducing the power consumption of Data Center Networks (DCNs) and guaranteeing the Flow Completion Time (FCT) of applications in DCNs are two major concerns for data center operators. However, existing works cannot realize the two goals together because of two issues: (1) The dynamic traffic pattern in DCNs is hard to accurately formulate; (2) An optimal flow scheduling scheme is complicated and computationally expensive.

In this paper, we propose SmartFCT, which employs the Deep Reinforcement Learning (DRL) coupled with Software-Defined Networking (SDN) to improve the power efficiency of DCNs and guarantee the FCT. SmartFCT dynamically collects the traffic distribution from the data plane. The DRL agent of SmartFCT can quickly analyze the complicated traffic characteristics using neural networks and generate a dynamic action output for traffic scheduling. Based on the action the traffic is consolidated into fewer active links and switches for power efficiency. At the same time there leaves different margins in different active links and devices to avoid FCT violation of unexpected burst of flows. Simulation results show that SmartFCT can guarantee the FCT and save up to 12.2% more power consumption than the state-of-the-art solutions.
